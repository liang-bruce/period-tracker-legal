# Privacy Policy for Period Tracker

_Last updated: 2026-05-09_

## What we collect

Period Tracker stores period-tracking data (start dates, end dates, cycle settings, language preference) **only on your device** and in **your personal iCloud account**. We — the developer — do not collect, transmit, or have access to any of your data.

- **Period records** (start date, end date) are saved using Apple's SwiftData framework.
- **Settings** (period length, cycle length, language) are saved using Apple's `UserDefaults`.
- **iCloud sync**: if you have iCloud enabled on your Apple devices, your data is synced privately through Apple's CloudKit service to your own iCloud account so the same data is available across your iPhone, iPad, and Apple Watch. We have no access to this iCloud data.

## What we do NOT collect

- We do not collect personal information, location, contacts, photos, or any other data outside the app.
- We do not use analytics, tracking SDKs, advertising identifiers, or third-party services.
- We do not share or sell any data — there is no data leaving your devices/iCloud to share or sell.

## Tip jar

If you choose to leave a tip via in-app purchase, the transaction is handled entirely by Apple. We receive Apple's standard payout report (anonymized, aggregated). We do not see your name, email, payment details, or which specific tip you chose.

## Your control

- All your period data lives on your device and in your iCloud. You can delete it at any time by deleting individual entries in the History tab, or by uninstalling the app and removing app data from iCloud (Settings → [Your Name] → iCloud → Manage Storage → Period Tracker → Delete Data).

## Children's privacy

The app is not directed at children under 13 and does not knowingly collect any data from anyone.
