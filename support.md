## Frequently Asked Questions

### How do I record a period?
On the Today tab, tap **Start Period** when your period begins. When it ends, open the app again and tap **End Period**. That's it.

### How do I change the language?
Tap the menu icon (≡) in the top-right of the Today tab → choose your preferred language under **Language**.

### How do I change the default period length or cycle length?
Menu icon (≡) → adjust **Period Length** and **Cycle Length** with the steppers. These settings are used for future calculations.

### Where is my data stored?
All your data is stored locally on your device and (if you have iCloud enabled) synced privately through your own iCloud account to your other Apple devices. The developer never has access to your data.

### My iPhone, iPad, and Apple Watch don't sync. Why?
Make sure:
1. All devices are signed into the same iCloud account (Settings → [Your Name]).
2. iCloud Drive is enabled.
3. Each device is connected to the internet.
4. Wait a minute or two — CloudKit sync isn't instant.

If sync still doesn't work, please email me.

### How do I edit or delete a past entry?
Open the History tab → swipe left on the entry → tap **Edit** or **Delete**.

### How do I filter history?
The History tab includes a date range filter at the top. Set the From/To dates to see entries that overlap with that range.

### What about the tip jar?
The Support / Buy Me a Coffee option in the menu is completely optional. Tips help keep the app free and ad-free. There are no features locked behind tips.

### Will my data be lost if I uninstall the app?
Local data on the uninstalled device will be lost, but data synced to iCloud remains on your other devices and in iCloud itself. To fully wipe iCloud data, go to Settings → [Your Name] → iCloud → Manage Storage → Just Period Tracker → Delete Data.
